Our Services

Retinal photography

Retinal photography

At our Plymstock and Saltash branches we offer the facility of retinal photography.


This allows us to photograph the back of the eye in great detail, which enables us to monitor existing conditions such as diabetes and glaucoma, as well as detect early signs of eye disease.


The photos are stored so that the optician can monitor any changes in future visits if necessary.

Frames

Frames

Being an independent opticians, we are able to stock a large range of spectacle frames, from modern budget to top end designer frames and everything inbetween.


Amongst the ranges available are: Gant, Guess, Stepper, Flexon, Jaguar, Maggy rouf, Cazal, Humphreys, Storm, Brendel, Rip curl and Schott.


If you have a particular shape or design in mind, and we do not have it in stock, we are always happy to try and get them in for you to view on approval with no commitment to buy.
